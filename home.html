Royal college Colombo

About Us

The History Club was founded in 1976 with the intent of enhancing and popularizing historical knowledge within the school with the aim of observing the past to secure and improve our own future. For this the History Club organizes seminars, workshops and quizzes across the year for students from across the country.

Weekly Facts`23

The “Memoirs of Serendib ’23” is a series of field trips to several National Museums in the Colombo District, organized by the Royal College History Club. This is a project which was established in 2016 under the guidance of our present Teacher In-Charge, Mr. Palitha Abeywardena. The project will take place in April, July, August and September and we aim to visit one museum in each month. Our aim in this project is to increase the enthusiasm towards the subject History among the fellow Royalists.
